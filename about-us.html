About us

Welcome to Arvies, where time becomes an exquisite expression of style and sophistication. At Arvies, we believe that a watch is more than just a timekeeping device, it's a reflection of your personality, an embodiment of craftsmanship, and a testament to enduring elegance.

  • Our Story

    Arvies was born out of a passion for horology and a desire to redefine the way we perceive timepieces. Established in 1986, we set out on a journey to curate a collection of watches that seamlessly blend timeless design with contemporary flair. Each Arvies timepiece is a testament to precision engineering and a commitment to quality.

  • Our Mission

    Our mission at Arvies is to offer more than just watches; we aim to provide an experience, a moment frozen in time that accompanies you through life's milestones. We strive to create watches that not only adorn your wrist but also tell a story, sparking conversations and creating lasting memories.

  • Craftsmanship

    Arvies are meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who share our dedication to excellence. From the intricate details of the dial to the seamless movement of the hands, every element is thoughtfully designed to elevate your timekeeping experience.

  • Sustainability

    We are committed to sustainable practices in our production processes. Arvies Watches are not just a fashion statement; they represent our commitment to ethical and environmentally conscious manufacturing.
